01 // OVERVIEW

Who We Are

The Russian Army is a Modern Military clan, founded by NerfModder on March 12, 2012. We're a hybrid group, standing at the crossing of the military roleplay and clanning communities.

Our goal is simple: build an enjoyable, close-knit community for everyone — especially those who share our love for Russian technology and hardware.

Many of our games draw from the biggest names in the genre — Call of Duty and Battlefield — borrowing their gamemodes, maps, and features to build experiences our members already know how to love.
